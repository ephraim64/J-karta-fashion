J'karta

J’KARTA is an international Ghanaian fashion brand which focuses on manufacturing premium eco-friendly products and accessories.The brand mainly uses both recycled and new burlap in producing some of the finest sustainable unisex clothes, shoes, bags and other fashion accessories

History and Scope

The word J’KARTA means ‘My Sack’, and is translated from the popular West African native Hausa language. The brand’s birth was motivated by the belief in the protection of the environment and the essence of a greener world, where products such as jute sacks (burlap), car tyres, pre-owned jeans, woollen materials, and leather can be recycled into beautifully crafted clothes, bags and shoes. J’KARTA further aimed at creating a sack collection made by Ghanaians, for Ghanaians and the rest of the world, to exhibit the creativeness and artistic nature of Africans.

It has been in the business of manufacturing jute products since its establishment in 2019, initially starting off with unique home and interior design works. J’KARTA’s objectives quickly spread across providing customers with quality and affordable unisex fashion accessories, eventually attracting the attention of many including Africans in the diaspora.

AIMS, OBJECTIVES AND PURPOSE

Apart from J’KARTA’s core mandate of providing quality and environmentally-friendly fashion products to all, the business was also set up to support skilled but disadvantaged youth in the area of fashion. Our Mission

Our goal is to be a recognized enabler of the sustainable fashion industry maintain its production of apparels mainly from eco-friendly materials. J’KARTA also aims at becoming a world class premium fashion brand that continues to make high quality clothes, shoes, bags and other accessories, which Africans around the world can feel proud of. J’KARTA also aims at acquiring much more sophisticated machinery and space for production in mass quantities, as well as various other whole retail outlets for distribution in Ghana and beyond.
